About us

Founded in 2024, HerVoice was born out of a collective desire to combat the pervasive issue of gender-based violence. Our founders, inspired by personal experiences and a deep commitment to social justice, set out to create an app that not only offers practical safety tools but also fosters a supportive community. Through partnerships with local authorities, law enforcement, and community organizations, HerVoice is dedicated to making our communities safer for everyone


  • Mission
  • Vision

At HerVoice, our mission is to empower individuals and communities to stand against gender-based violence through innovative technology and collective action.

Our vision is a world where safety and security are guaranteed for all individuals, regardless of gender. We envision communities where everyone, particularly women and girls who are disproportionately affected by gender-based violence, can live without fear.

Focus on Female Gender

While HerVoice is designed to support all individuals, we place a particular focus on women and girls. This focus stems from the stark reality that women and girls are disproportionately affected by gender-based violence. They face higher risks and more severe consequences, making it imperative to provide targeted support and resources.

Statistics and Impact: Women and girls are more likely to experience various forms of gender-based violence, including domestic abuse, sexual assault, and harassment. The physical and psychological impacts of gender-based violence on women and girls are profound, often leading to long-term health issues and economic instability.
